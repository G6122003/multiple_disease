Know Your Health Risks

Our web application uses advanced Machine Learning algorithms to help users predict four major diseases effectively: Diabetes, Heart Disease, Breast Cancer, and Kidney Disease.

This system is designed to be used by both everyday individuals and researchers for better health monitoring and early disease detection.

Key Features:

  • Analyzes critical health parameters to detect risks early
  • Supports preventive healthcare through fast and accurate predictions
  • Built with Flask and integrated with trained machine learning models
  • High accuracy, user-friendly design, and meaningful output interpretation
  • Useful for both normal users and researchers in healthcare analytics

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate are the predictions?

Our system uses trained machine learning models with high accuracy rates, but predictions should be used as guidance and not definitive diagnosis. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Can I use this system for early detection?

Yes, the system is designed to analyze risk factors and provide early indications of potential health issues, encouraging timely medical consultation.

Is my data secure?

All user inputs are processed locally or securely transmitted. We prioritize your privacy and do not store personal health data without consent.
